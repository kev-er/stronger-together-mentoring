About Emma...

Author, Wife, Mother;

About Emma ...I am a trained life coach and mentor and accredited by the European Mentoring and Coaching Confederation and am based in the Woking area. I meet the standards of senior practitioner and have particular expertise in the area of relationships.

I am a wife of 29 years and I have 3 grown up daughters. My story is one of extreme challenges, particularly in marriage issues and parenting, as my youngest has special needs and severe learning difficulties. I am the author of the book STRETCHED which was published in 2019. I have training and experience in parent classes and have worked with teenagers in alternative education settings. I am currently studying a masters in spiritual formation.
