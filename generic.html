About me

Author, Wife, Mother;

About Emma ... Emma is passionate about helping people unlock their potential to live out their dreams and desires. Emma offers mentoring to individuals and groups which will equip and empower you to feel more in control and to make changes that make you feel lighter for life's adventure. She is the author of Stretched, which tells her story of hope and survival through many challenges. Emma has trained experience in parenting classes and personal experience of parenting her own child with additional needs. Her extensive experience in mentoring has given her skills that are fresh and relevant to any age, gender and stage of life. She has expertise in relationship restoration, navigating special needs, working 1:1 with disengaged teenagers and support for parents.
