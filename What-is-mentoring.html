What is mentoring?

  • Mentoring is a service that supports you to make changes in your life.

  • It can be done online or in person, where we sit and have conversation about your subject for 50 minutes- 1 hour.

  • We contract to discuss and be open in a non-judgemental relationship where you share your subject and I listen and ask questions that can help you see more around your subject.

  • Mentoring deals with current and future life aspects. It is not focussed on past events, although they may be referred to, to understand the context of the current issues.

  • Mentoring helps to bring greater self-awareness of your world around you.

  • The mentoring relationship usually lasts at least 6 weeks but can go on as long as a few years.
